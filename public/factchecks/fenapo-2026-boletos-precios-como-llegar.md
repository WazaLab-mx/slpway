**CLAIM:** "Para el resto del cartel las terrazas van de ~$200 a $600 MXN por sección según la misma fuente."

**INVESTIGATION SUMMARY:**
The floor is right but the ceiling is materially understated, and the attribution to El Universal SLP does not match what that source's full table shows. **EVIDENCE CHAIN:**
- **Primary Source:** [El Universal SLP — Costo de las Terrazas en la Fenapo 2026 (Jun 17/19, 2026)](https://sanluis.eluniversal.com.mx/mas-de-san-luis/katy-perry-y-motley-crue-en-slp-costo-de-las-terrazas-en-la-fenapo-2026-para-los-conciertos/) — full table: rest-of-lineup Terraza Verde spans $200–$1,800 and Terraza Blanca $400–$2,500
- **Counter-Evidence (from the same source):** Gloria Trevi $1,800/$2,500 and Los Tigres del Norte $1,000/$2,000 — non-headliner dates far above the stated $600 ceiling

A reader budgeting for Gloria Trevi or Los Tigres del Norte terraza seats based on the post's "$200–$600" line would be off by a factor of 3–4x. **CONFIDENCE:** High — the discrepancy is internal to the post's own cited source.
